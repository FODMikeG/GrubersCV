My first experience with computers dates all the way back to around 1989 when I played Number muncher in class the first time. Back then it was in AP Math that we were afforded the opportunity to see our first computers. It was then that I fell in love with the PC and what it could do. From that moment on I have spent my life building my own computers over the years and doing my best to keep up with rapidly growing technology.

I have always wanted to learn to code and become a major contributor to this world but back when I was young the ability to code was seen more as illegal hackers or government types trying to stop them. So I resolved myself to learn a bit of HTML, a bit of JAVA and gaming. For a long time I forgot about my dream to code and ran my own LLC making a fair bit of money in the process.

Now in 2024 I find that there are classes teaching software engineering and coding. So it is time to fulfill my dream of becoming a coder. I have signed up for USF, coding bootcamp to get the ball rolling and look forward to kicking down the doors of the coding world!
